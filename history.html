BARANGAY CABOG HISTORY


During the Japanese occupation the place is a part of Barrio Tubod.

The place traversed by two streams namely Cabog and Anislagan Creeks, both side of the streams growned a natural growing trees like Tuog,Anislag and etc.

There were bats in vernacular ( CABOG ) stay during night time.

Then the Barangay folks wants to separate from mother Barangay Tubod

CABOG was originated by the Polinar Family, Silagan Family,Enoc Family,Patac Family,Boton Family,Fuentes Family,Bascon Family.

This Family joined hand in hand to formed a new Barrio name CABOG on June 26, 1948.
